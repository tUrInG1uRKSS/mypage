About Me

Hello! I am Leandro Llontop Herrera, a Mechatronics Engineer and Robotics enthusiast driven by the control of complex systems and mechatronic integration.

I am currently pursuing a Master’s in Robotics, Automation & Embedded Systems at IMT Atlantique in Nantes, France. My academic journey spans across Peru, Germany, and France, giving me a unique international perspective and a versatile problem-solving approach.

What Drives Me?

I seek to contribute to innovative projects that combine control, perception, and will change the world.

Beyond Engineering

I believe in discipline and teamwork, values I cultivate through sports. I am an active Football & Futsal player. I am also passionate about Formula 1 (for the engineering and strategy!), cinema, and table tennis.

I am actively looking for an internship opportunity (4+ months) starting in April 2025. If you are looking for a rigorous, curious, and adaptable engineer, let’s connect!

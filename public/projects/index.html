LabVIEW NI DAQ Analog Electronics (LM741 AD633) MATLAB/Simulink Proteus Nonlinear Control Theory

Digital implementation of Feedback Linearization vs. Simple Linearization control strategies on a physically modeled Van der Pol oscillator using OpAmps and multipliers. Feedback Linearization proved superior with a 1.2s settling time and only 5% overshoot, implemented via NI DAQ and LabVIEW.
